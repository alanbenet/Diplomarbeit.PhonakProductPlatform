AutoSense OS 4.0 - significantly less listening effort and preferred for speech intelligibility

12/30/2020 -  

This unique remote study was carried out in times of Covid-19 at the Hörzentrum Oldenburg in Germany. Study participants received a ‘Remote Hearing Lab’ containing test equipment, instructions and a laptop for video-calling with the tester. Using this remote testing, we were able to show that our Paradise hearing aids with AutoSense OS 4.0 are preferred regarding speech intelligibility and clients report significantly less listening effort.

More evidence for Phonak Paradise hearing aids – there is nothing like the sound of paradise…

11/06/2020 -  

Talk while you walk, with Paradise hearing aids! This brand new study, conducted in Canada evaluated the algorithm Motion Sensor Hearing in Phonak Paradise hearing aids. Participants tested Motion Sensor Hearing while listening to a talker whilst walking on a busy street. Participants preferred Motion Sensor Hearing in terms of speech understanding, environmental awareness, overall listening experience and sound quality.

Phonak is introducing Audéo M-312T and Audéo M-13T hearing aids with T-coil. 11/06/2020 - New SlimTip Titanium improves durability and fit rate

Phonak is introducing its first ever SlimTip Titanium with Marvel hearing aids. Following in the footsteps of the award-winning Virto B-Titanium, the new SlimTip Titanium is made from medical grade titanium. It is 15X stronger than industry standard acrylic, hypoallergenic and IP68-rated. It was designed to withstand the inner ear environment, and is comfortable to wear. The strength of titanium allows Phonak 3D modelers to design and 3D print Titanium SlimTip as thin as 0.2mm. The new SlimTip Titanium has been proven to increase fit rate for smaller, more challenging ears. You can now offer your clients the choice of acrylic, silicone, or Titanium SlimTips based on preference for comfort and fit.
